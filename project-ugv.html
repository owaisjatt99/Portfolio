Metal Detection UGV

An autonomous Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) designed for hazardous environment scanning, featuring GPS tracking and GSM alerting.

Chassis & Mobility

Robust framework designed for varied terrain traversal with high-torque motor drivers.

  • Custom 4WD chassis for stable mobility.
  • L298N high-performance motor drivers.
  • Off-road wheels for high clearance scanning.

Electromagnetic Sensing

Advanced metal detection system utilizing electromagnetic induction principles.

  • Custom-wound induction coils for high sensitivity.
  • Buzzer feedback and alert indicators.
  • Precise ground scanning for buried metallic objects.

Arduino Core Integration

The neural hub of the UGV, managing all sensor data and autonomous logic.

  • Arduino Uno/Mega for real-time processing.
  • Embedded C logic for obstacle avoidance.
  • Integrated sensor orchestration for UGV health.

GSM & GPS Telemetry

Real-time location tracking and immediate SMS alerts upon metal detection.

  • Neo-6M GPS modules for precise coordinates.
  • SIM800L for global GSM connectivity.
  • Instant SMS alerts sent to the control center.
